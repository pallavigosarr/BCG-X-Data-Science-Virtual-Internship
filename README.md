# BCG X Data Science Virtual Internship Program

## Project Overview

This project was completed as part of the BCG X Data Science Virtual Experience Program on Forage. The objective was to analyze customer and pricing data for an energy company and develop a machine learning solution to predict customer churn.

The project simulates the work of a data scientist at BCG X, involving business understanding, exploratory data analysis, feature engineering, predictive modeling, and business recommendations.

---

## Business Problem

Customer churn is a major challenge for energy providers. Acquiring new customers is often more expensive than retaining existing ones. The company wanted to identify customers at risk of leaving and understand the key factors influencing churn.

---

## Project Objectives

* Analyze customer and pricing data.
* Identify patterns associated with customer churn.
* Engineer relevant features for modeling.
* Build a machine learning model to predict churn.
* Provide actionable business recommendations.

---

## Tasks Completed

### Task 1: Business Understanding

* Understood the business context.
* Identified factors potentially affecting churn.
* Formulated analytical questions to guide the investigation.

### Task 2: Exploratory Data Analysis (EDA)

* Examined customer and pricing datasets.
* Checked data types and data quality.
* Handled missing values and inconsistencies.
* Generated summary statistics.
* Created visualizations to identify trends and outliers.

### Task 3: Feature Engineering & Modeling

* Created new features from existing customer data.
* Prepared data for machine learning.
* Split data into training and testing datasets.
* Built a Random Forest classification model.
* Evaluated model performance using standard classification metrics.

### Task 4: Executive Summary

* Interpreted model findings.
* Identified key drivers of churn.
* Presented business recommendations to improve customer retention.

## Key Insights

* Customer behavior and contract characteristics influence churn probability.
* Historical usage patterns provide valuable predictive signals.
* Machine learning can help identify high-risk customers before they leave.

---

## Business Recommendations

* Identify customers with high churn probability.
* Offer targeted retention discounts only to those customers.
* Monitor churn reduction and retrain the model periodically.
* Offer loyalty incentives for long-term contracts.
* Improve customer engagement through proactive account management.
* Introduce more stable and predictable pricing plans.
